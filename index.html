My Story

Coding with Purpose

I am a full stack developer with a background of 13 years in pastoral ministry and healthcare chaplaincy (currently working in hospice). I trained in Full Stack Web Development with the University of Arizona and my skills include MongoDB, MySql, Express, React.js, Node, Heroku, Github, JQuery, Bootstrap, Handlebars, responsive web design, and more. I also have a Masters in Divinity and 4 units of Clinical Pastoral Education. All throughout my career I have found ways to use technology to engage people in the most important questions of life. I am an ideal candidate to bring both technical and relational skills to a programming team.

This site is a simple, one-page design. The top of the page shows a list of the burgers. The user may "eat the burger" and move it into the eaten column, or decide to "make it again." On the bottom of the site the user can input new burgers. This data persists and is maintained by a MySQL database. The deployed site is hosted through Heroku, and the dynamically created content is handled via Handlebars. The github repository for this project may be found at: https://github.com/Ayeser/BurgerPicker
